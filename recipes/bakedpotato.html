Baked Potato

Description

Have you dreamed of eating a baked potato? No? Well here's a recipe for one anyway!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium baking potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300. Scrub potato, and poke several times with knife or fork. Rub skin with olive oil (the potato's skin, not yours), then with salt (same thing).
  2. Put potato in oven and bake around 90 minutes. Slice down the center and serve with butter and black pepper. Add cheese if wanted.
