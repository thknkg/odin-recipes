Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

For when you're sick or you don't have ingredients to make something better!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 4 cans chicken broth
  • 1 can vegetable broth
  • 1/2 pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook til tinder (~5 minutes)
  2. Add remaining ingredients. Stir to combine and bring to boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
